Introduction

Aftab Engineering Services Pvt Ltd

Automotive and HVAC Components Manufacturer

Welcome to Aftab Engineering Services (Pvt.) Ltd. (AESL), a cornerstone of excellence in the engineering sector for over 35 years. Originally founded as Aftab Technologies (Pvt.) Ltd., our company has evolved with the dynamic needs of the automotive, HVAC, and home appliances industries. As a leading manufacturer of precision metal body parts for the automobile sector and high-quality HVAC components, AESL stands at the forefront of industry innovation. Our commitment to excellence and our ability to adapt to the rapidly growing demands of both sectors define our legacy and drive our future.
