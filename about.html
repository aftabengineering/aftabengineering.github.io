Introduction

Aftab Engineering Services Pvt Ltd

Automotive and HVAC Components Manufacturer

Welcome to Aftab Engineering Services (Pvt.) Ltd. (AESPL), a cornerstone of excellence in the engineering sector for over 35 years. Originally founded as Aftab Technologies (Pvt.) Ltd., our company has evolved with the dynamic needs of the automotive, HVAC, and home appliances industries. As a leading manufacturer of precision metal body parts for the automobile sector and high-quality HVAC components, AESPL stands at the forefront of industry innovation. Our commitment to excellence and our ability to adapt to the rapidly growing demands of both sectors define our legacy and drive our future. Discover how AESPL's expertise and dedication continue to set benchmarks in engineering and manufacturing excellence.

Mission

Our mission is to carry on and further build upon the sophisticated machineries and excellent services as provided by the former Organization (Aftab Technologies) in order to satisfy our customers by providing best quality products, timely deliveries with most competitive prices.

Vision

To be recognized as a reputable manufacturing partners for Automotive & Engineering sector in Pakistan & Worldwide.

Aftab Engineering Services (Pvt.) Ltd., previously Aftab Technologies (Pvt.) Ltd., has delivered expert engineering solutions for 35 years, specializing in the automotive, HVAC, and home appliances industries.
